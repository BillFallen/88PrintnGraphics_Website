About Us

Who We Are

Eighty 8 Print & Graphics is a printing and branding company based in Francistown, Botswana. We specialize in delivering high-quality print solutions including business cards, flyers, banners, signage, and custom branding for businesses and individuals.

Our services include flyers, banners, business cards, signage, vehicle branding, event branding, product labels and other custom graphic design work.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, creative and professional printing and graphic design services that help businesses and individuals communicate clearly, advertise effectively and build a strong brand image.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the leading printing and branding companies in Botswana by delivering quality work, modern designs and excellent customer service.

Why Choose Us

  • High-quality printing and finishing
  • Creative and modern branding solutions
  • Professional customer service
  • Affordable and flexible service options
  • Reliable turnaround times

Our Location

Eighty 8 Print & Graphics is located in the Light Industrial Area in Francistown, Botswana, near/opposite Fours Warehouse and other surrounding businesses. This convenient location makes it easy for customers to visit us for printing, branding and design services.
