Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about our printing and branding services. If you need more information, feel free to contact us directly.

How long does printing take?

Turnaround time depends on the type and size of the order. Small print jobs can take 1-2 days, while larger or custom projects may take longer.

Do you offer graphic design services?

Yes, we provide professional graphic design services including logos, posters, flyers, branding materials and custom designs.

How can I request a quote?

You can request a quote by filling in the contact form on the Contact Us page or by calling or emailing us directly.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept common file formats such as PDF, PNG, JPG and editable design files. For best results, high-resolution files are recommended.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer installation services for signage, vehicle branding and other large projects depending on the client's needs.

Where are you located?

We are located in the Light Industrial Area in Francistown, Botswana, near Fours Warehouse and surrounding businesses.

Do you require a deposit before starting work?

Yes, a deposit may be required depending on the size and type of the project. This will be communicated during the quotation process.
